RobotMotion

A large-scale human behavior dataset for training physical AI and humanoid robots.

About

RobotMotion is a data collection platform and dataset focused on capturing real-world human actions, audio, and interactions to enable robot learning and behavior imitation.

Dataset Overview

RobotMotion is a large-scale dataset of real-world human behavior collected through egocentric video, audio, and task-oriented recordings.

  • Modalities: RGB video, audio, task metadata
  • Viewpoint: Egocentric (first-person)
  • Environments: Indoor & real-world daily settings
  • Granularity: Long-horizon action sequences

Supported Tasks

Manipulation, navigation, daily activities, tool usage, and long-horizon task execution.

Data Access

The RobotMotion dataset will be released in phases.

  • Phase 1: Public subset for research and benchmarking
  • Phase 2: Extended dataset under research agreement
  • Phase 3: Commercial access for enterprise use

Intended Use

RobotMotion is designed for research in robot learning, imitation learning, and physical AI. It is not intended for surveillance or biometric identification.
